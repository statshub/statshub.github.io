Statistical and Software Training Courses / Workshops

Have you got a member of staff or team that needs to develop their statistical thinking and data analysis skills? We can run either our standard courses or tailor-made courses based on your own data and problems on your premises.

The department also conducts workshops in Microsoft excel / SPSS or STATA without having to spend huge amounts of money on software. Notable contributions have also been made to all teaching programs and workshops organized by the department of Biostatistics or Clinical Epidemiology Unit (CEU) at Christian Medical College (CMC). It includes training of the staff, students and faculty from this institution and other institutions in India.

Statistical Support for Academic Research

STATSHUB Research consulting services can provide statistics help for academic researchers including undergraduate or postgraduate medical students (working on thesis, dissertations, PhD scholars)

Also for other academic researchers in the field of Psychology, Biochemistry, Microbiology, Physiology etc.

Other Services

In addition to the above services we do market research, Data management, Design of Experiments, Microsoft tools and Recruitment consultancy for Statistical roles.
